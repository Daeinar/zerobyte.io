Hello and welcome to my site! My name is Philipp Jovanovic.

I am a cryptographer and postdoctoral researcher at the Decentralized and Distributed Systems Lab at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland.

My research interests include applied cryotgraphy, IT-security, and digital privacy protection.

I completed my PhD in cryptology in 2015 at the Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics of the University of Passau, Germany. My PhD thesis, received the 2016 dissertation award of the University of Passau in the area of mathematics / computer science.
