About

Hi! This is Philipp. I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Decentralized and Distributed Systems Lab at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland. I completed my PhD in cryptology in 2015 at the Faculty of Computer Science and Mathematics of the University of Passau, Germany.

Among other topics, I am interested in applied cryptography, digital privacy protection, and IT-security. At the moment, I am in particular busy with the analysis and design of authenticated encryption schemes, distributed cryptographic protocols, and cryptocurrencies.
